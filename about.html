About me

Front-end developer from Rota, Saipan

Aloha! I'm an island girl at heart who loves adventure and self-growth. Born and raised on the tropical island of Rota in the Pacific, I live for those long sunny days spent hiking island trails and swimming in the crystal blue ocean. Though I left my island home to complete high school stateside, graduating early in the top 10%, I'll always be a water baby who feels most at peace listening to the rolling waves.

Nowadays you can find me exploring the great outdoors, reading books that transport me to new worlds, jamming to bachata and reggaeton, and chasing endless summer vibes. I balance my wanderlust spirit by engaging my mind - whether it's learning new things, solving problems or expressing my creativity through fashion and coding. I'm always down for meaningful conversations over sunsets and picnics in meadows. There's so much beauty to embrace in this world and I try to radiate the same warmth that my island home provides.
